Meal Advice

  • Always accompany each meal with a glass of water. Staying hydrated helps with digestion and can prevent overeating.
  • Avoid skipping meals, as it can lead to overeating later in the day.
  • Include a source of lean protein in every meal to support muscle growth and repair.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains for added fiber and nutrients.

Other Advice

  • Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise every day.
  • Practice mindful eating by savoring each bite and listening to your body's hunger and fullness cues.
  • Limit processed and sugary foods, as they often provide empty calories and little nutritional value.
  • Get adequate sleep to support overall health and maintain a healthy weight.
